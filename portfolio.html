Network Portfolio

This portfolio showcases my hands-on experience with network setups, including a robust home network with TP-Link Omada, a Cisco Lab for CCNA practice, and more. Key projects include:

  • Cisco Home Lab: Configured with L3/L2 switches, routers, and VLANs for CCNA prep, featuring full CLI configurations and troubleshooting.
  • TP-Link Omada Home Network: A multi-AP setup with a PTP bridge connecting two buildings, supporting Wi-Fi, cameras, and renter access. Upgraded with managed switches (TL-SG3210XHP-M2) and planning outdoor APs.
